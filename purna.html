Strategic Focus

Tourism of Karnali Province

Karnali Province contains an unmatched potential for pristine heritage exploration, high-altitude trekking, and deep experiential hospitality models that remain largely unmapped.

Our ongoing initiatives focus entirely on activating structured economic loops that support off-grid mountain homestays, empower regional guides, and implement rigid environmental protection standards.
